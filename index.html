About BeamNG.drive

BeamNG.drive is a driving simulation game that combines stunning physics, open-world gameplay, and an extensive modding community. It allows players to experience true-to-life driving mechanics in an immersive sandbox environment. From realistic crash simulations to custom-built maps, BeamNG.drive offers endless possibilities for creativity and exploration.

Whether you're a casual player who loves to explore or a hardcore simulation enthusiast, BeamNG.drive caters to all. The game's soft-body physics engine is a masterpiece, simulating every dent and deformation of your vehicle. The community is thriving with thousands of mods, from custom vehicles to unique terrains. BeamNG.drive is more than a game; it's a platform for your imagination.

Features

  • Realistic soft-body physics for accurate vehicle dynamics.
  • Extensive vehicle and map library for diverse gameplay experiences.
  • Support for mods and user-generated content.
  • Multiplayer functionality for playing with friends.
  • Dynamic weather and time-of-day settings for enhanced immersion.
  • Regular updates introducing new content and features.
